How I work

Simple commission process

Clear steps help avoid confusion and make the commission easier for both sides.

1

Explain the system

Tell me what you need, what UI/models you already have, and your budget.

2

I plan the logic

I break down the system into RemoteEvents, server logic, client UI, and saving if needed.

3

I script and test

I build the system and test important cases like cooldowns, deaths, resets, mobile, and spam.

4

You review it

You test it in your game and we polish anything that needs adjustment.
